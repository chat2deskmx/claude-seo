## §7. Scripts deep-dive (30 files, 14,426 LOC)

### Cross-cutting patterns (most → least frequent)

| Pattern | Affected scripts | Severity | Fix template |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bare `except:` / `except Exception:` | 15 scripts (50%) | MEDIUM | Replace with specific exceptions: `except (json.JSONDecodeError, KeyError, ValueError) as e:` |
| Hardcoded threshold/limit constants | 8 scripts (27%) | MEDIUM | Extract to module-level constants with env override: `THRESHOLD = int(os.getenv('LCP_THRESHOLD', '75'))` |
| Missing `encoding='utf-8'` on `open()` | 5 scripts (17%) | MEDIUM | Always `open(path, 'r', encoding='utf-8')` — fails on non-UTF-8 locales |
| In-memory only quota tracking | 4 scripts (13%) | MEDIUM | Persist in SQLite or shared lockfile (see `dataforseo_costs.py` for partial pattern) |
